Dots represent a number that is equal to itself when multiplied in modulo

Coloring Algorithms:

  • Black: all lines are black, no coloring is applied
  • By Length: lines are colored with hue based on their length in proportion to the circle diameter
  • By Loops: lines that are part of a loop get a color per loop, the ones that point into a loop node but aren't in a loop themselves are black (this may be quite slow for large moduli)

By RubenVerg. Source code available on GitHub.
